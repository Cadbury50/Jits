ABOUT US

JITS CAFE SHOP creates a cafe shop without harmful chemicals and heavy processing, ingredients from farmers we know, and ingredients that respects the environment. It's the same type of Cafe shop that inspired our founder, Enrico Salavarria, to start JITS CAFE SHOP.

The name "JITS CAFE" is meant to convey a diner that can be excited about because of its sustainable values and practices. It's a concept that leads Enrico to create a cafe shop and made a difference in people's lives. While the name came quickly from love; a family love especially to his wife and son, that serves as the inspiration and strength of a man like Enrico. Enrico hope to improve the game. He knew that he'd have to do more than just serve drinks that were better for the costumers; he’d have to serve drinks that are better for the environment and for the farmers. And so here we are today. We hope the world is just a little better with us around and that you'll join us in helping to MAKE EVERYBODY HAPPY!

VISSION: To bring a unique taste of happiness to our customers through quality, creative drinks and extraordinary cafe experience while working toward the greater good for our employees, community and environment.

MISSION: To sell delicious and remarkable drinks to reach people's heart and to consistently provide our customers with impeccable service by letting them taste the creativeness and exceptional cafe experience.
